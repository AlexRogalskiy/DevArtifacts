How to use

Draw your art then use the thumbnail at the bottom-right to export the flattened 32x32 .png to a new tab for saving. The app can not save your in-progress work, only export flat .png files.

Color presets will be remembered on a HTML5 LocalStorage-capable browser. This app has 10 levels of undo.

About

HARDCELL was hacked together by @njmcode for a laugh as inspiration/procrastination for #LOWREZJAM 2014. The underlying code is grim and needs a full refactor whenever time permits.

This app may well not function correctly on anything other than Chrome stable. Sorry about that.
