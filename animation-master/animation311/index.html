Badtz-Maru

In Japanese, "Badtz" is a term for "X" and "Maru" indicates a circle or "O", which together means wrong-correct.

  • Birthday: April 1, 1993
  • Species: Bird
  • Residence: Gorgeoustown
  • Hobbies: Eye rolling

Sanrio debuted Badtz Maru in 1993. He is a penguin with attitude and is often referred to as "XO". His dream is to become the boss of everything!
