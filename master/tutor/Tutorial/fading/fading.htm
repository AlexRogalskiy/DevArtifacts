PhotoLine Tip: Remove red eyes

1. You have a photo where the eyes of a person got red because of the flash of your camera. 

2. To change the color of the eyes, select the tool Replace Colors from the Tool Bar.

3. Setup the Tool Options to about 60% Tolerance and select Click Color -> Foreground Color. This function replaces the color under the mouse when you click into the image by the foreground color. So your next step should be to setup the foreground color to your new eye color (brown, blue or green).

4. Use a little unsharp pen and draw around the red part of the eyes and you will get a nice photo.
