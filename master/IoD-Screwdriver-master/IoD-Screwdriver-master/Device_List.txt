This is the list of devices detected by the IoD-Screwdriver plugin.

Some have been verified with the actual devices wherever possible.  

Others are extracted from the apps and other documentation sources and may include yet unreleased devices which may change in the future. Some 
devices also have several possible device names and so have multiple entries. Finally, some software may have had references to names where 
that were only partial or for unknown devices (prototypes, OEM, etc)

This list is far from definitive as there may be name colissions with non IoD devices.  However, it should give you a good idea how many 
devices are there.

100 device names from ~80 devices as of November 20, 2017

Any additions or corrections, please send them to screwdriver@internetofdon.gs
