Just for a laugh ;)

A layout based on the 'old fashioned HTML Frames' where the header and left column stay fixed, along with the background, and the content scrolls.

This allows the navigation to stay on screen at all times.

This works in IE6 (surprise!), NN7, Mozilla, Firefox and Opera.

Fails to work in IE5.x where the whole page scrolls.

A bit of jiggery-pokery with the images and z-index allow the content to scroll over the shadows but under the main image.
Scrolls smoothly in IE6 and Opera, but is a bit jerky in Mozilla, NN7 and Firefox.
IE5.x scrolls the whole page.

The only link that works is the 'Comments' one. Please feel free to leave any comments here, ta.
