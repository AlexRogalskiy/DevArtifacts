A SIMPLE 'MINIMUM WIDTH' LAYOUT

Hover over the arrows for a small preview image, then click on the arrow for a full size image with caption. The usual dotted line active/focus border will not be seen when the mouse is clicked.

This is also a demonstration of a basic minimum width layout that works in all browsers including IE. Resize your browser window and see the width shrink from 90% to a minimum of 700px, after which scroll bars will be added in all browsers - hopefully ;o)

It also has a transparent content background!
