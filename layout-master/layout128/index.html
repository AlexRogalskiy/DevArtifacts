Chris' Four-Bean Chili

We're gonna make ...

Directions

  • Get your filthiest campfire-scorched Dutch oven over the fire the best you can situate it. Brown the bison. Add the diced onion and spices, sautee until soft.
  • Add tomatoes and broth and bring to a simmer for 40 minutes. Add beans and cook another 30 minutes.

  • After that, taste, and adjust salt, pepper, and chili powder if necessary. If you have other spicy ingredients that suit you best, go for it.
  • The longer the chili simmers, the better it will taste. Remove from heat and serve, or refrigerate, and serve the next day.
  • Serve with Tabasco and a giant spoon.
