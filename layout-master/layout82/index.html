fullWidthImage()

Full Bleed Images on Percentage Width Content

Sometimes you need to have a big ol' image all up in your page. If your content's container has a percentage width, you can use this mixin to break the image out of the container.

Check out the image below.

Look at that bad ass image now!

Currently, this only works for image with percentage widths.
