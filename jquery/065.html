Test HTML5 support with Modernizr

Although not part of jQuery, the Modernizr library is an indispensable tool in your toolkit that you can use to determine how much of HTML5 the current browser supports.

Code

// What does your browser support? log('Custom fonts:', Modernizr.fontface); log('Border radius:', Modernizr.borderradius); log('Multiple Background Images:', Modernizr.multiplebgs); log('CSS Animations:', Modernizr.cssanimations); log('CSS Reflections:', Modernizr.cssreflections); log('CSS 3D Transformations:', Modernizr.csstransforms3d); log('Canvas:', Modernizr.canvas); log('Drag&Drop:', Modernizr.draganddrop); log('Audio:', Modernizr.audio); log('IndexedDB:', Modernizr.indexeddb); log('Web Workers:', Modernizr.webworkers); log('Geolocation:', Modernizr.geolocation); log('Touch Events:', Modernizr.touch); log('Webgl:', Modernizr.webgl);
