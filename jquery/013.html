Make your stylesheets editable

Your inline stylesheets are not set in stone. They can be made visible and even edited with a small jQuery snippet:

Code

// Make the stylesheet visible and editable $('#regular-style-block').css({'display':'block', 'white-space':'pre'}) .attr('contentEditable',true);
